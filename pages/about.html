The Beginning

Where It All
Began

Founded in Uganda in 2026, Mistry Scent was born from a passion for capturing fleeting moments in a bottle. Our master perfumer creates each fragrance using only the finest ingredients sourced from around the world.

We believe perfume is an invisible accessory that completes your personal style. Each fragrance is meticulously crafted to tell a story and evoke emotion, blending African heritage with modern sophistication.

Milestones of Mastery
